How to Export Your ChatGPT Data

1

Log in to ChatGPT

Go to chat.openai.com and log in to your ChatGPT account in your browser

2

Export Your Data

Click on your profile in the bottom left corner, then select Settings → Data Controls → Export Data

3

Upload to ChatRecap

After receiving the email, download and unzip the file. Upload the conversations.json file here to get your recap!
